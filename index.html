About Us

Shine.Rich Seafood locate in Jakarta - Indonesia which is under PT. Hoki Sinar Sukses proven as seafood trader of fresh and frozen seafood, transacting business with many food processors and wholesalers in Asia and Indonesia. Its run from small family business in Medan from 2005 with our own fleets. We do processing of some frozen products such as Squid, Cuttlefish and some other fish products major in exporting seafoods. Then In 2017, we expanded in trading some of imports fish such as Fresh & Frozen Salmon locally – buy from local company which doing Import directly. Under the flag name of PT. Hoki Sinar Sukses year 2018, we distribute our products both fresh and frozen to around Indonesia such as Kalimantan, Sumatras, Java, and Bali. We supply more than 10-20tons per month of Salmon and Trouts, Shrimps, Tuna, Squid Tube and others imports Japanese fish to restaurants and distributions channel in Indonesia. We are recognized and preferred by domestic and international suppliers, exporters and customers for its good quality. Also, it is known for the way it provides the best possible service at very competitive and Fair market prices, a result of Shine.Rich Seafood being a small yet very flexible and motivated team with low overheads and quick decision-making process. With a team that combines more than 20 years of experience in the seafood industry, Shine.Rich Seafood puts together its expertise and close relationships with production facilities to become a serious and trustworthy partner. In the process, we are able to offer a wide range of different products mainly in fresh To Retail customers wholesalers, distributors, and industrial customers. Our target markets have come to rely on us, knowing that they can get “sea premium quality” from us.

Our Mission

Based on a good, reliable and mutual relationships, we provide our customers with high quality, straightforward and sustainable fish and seafood products. Production in conformity with International standards, innovative generation with competitive and fair market prices for fresh and delicious seafoods.

Our Vission

Our Vission are to offer and distribute to our customers tasteful, healthy and ethical , competitive and fair market prices for fish product. And to be LEADER / TOP of Indonesia’s Seafood Market specialty in Niche Market on 3 years times. Building good relationships forms the basis on which we operates with our teams but also with our customers and suppliers. To this end, Shine.Rich Seafood – PT. HOKI SINAR SUKSES works exclusively with reliable partners in the fishing industries both from local and around the world.

Local Tuna
